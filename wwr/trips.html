Our Most Popular Trips

Suitable for those who want to just spend one perfect day on the river! You will start your day bright and early with breakfast by the water. Before launching, our guides will get you set up with all the gear you need and give you instructions for how to best manage the river. Once that is finished, you will be on your way down the river with stops along the way to refuel and rest. You'll end your day at our landing down river and the be brought back to the launch area via our vans to enjoy an all you can eat barbecue and entertainment.

If a little adventure and a lot of tranquility is more your style, this trip might be just right for you! After a leisurely morning, enjoying breakfast near our launch area, our guides will get everyone all set with their gear and give instructions. Then you'll be off for an hour long ride to Beaver Beach. You'll have the options of swimming, sitting on the shore or exploring along the river's edge until lunch. After that, our vans will pick you up and deliver you back to our main launch area, rested and ready for your evening.

Calling all thrill seekers and adventurers! Our 4 day High Adventure Trips are not to be missed! This all inclusive trip will provide you with gear, food, and some of the most amazing rafting around. Each day will be spent on the river and each evening you will camp at preset locations where there will be time to fish, swim or just enjoy the beaches. Our staff will have camp set up in advance, so after your long days on the river, you won't have to do more work! It's all the adventure without all the work...except paddling!
