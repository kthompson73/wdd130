Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote the Whitewater Adventures Tour company. This site will streamline the reservation process and provide more information than what is in the printed materials. Both things should help increase reveue.

Audience

Who is the target customer?
The target customer is anyone who loves outdoor adventures, specifically whitewater rafting, and can afford to travel and pay for adventure tours.

What will customers find on this website?
Customers will be able to find all of the information they need about a whitewater advernture tour. There will be photos of the areas they will be rafting through and the rafts. Information will be provided regarding fitness level and accessibility. There will be detailed prices lists as well as the ability to book all of their adventures online.

How will customers access this site?
Customers will access this site from a variety of devices, including tablets, phones, laptops, and desktops.
